Our Story

A coastline retreat, perfected over a quarter century.

Founded in 1998 on a private stretch of Mediterranean shore, Diamond Aurora Club was designed for guests who measure value in how a place makes them feel, not what it puts on display. We opened with six rooms, a kitchen, and a small bar. Today we have one hundred and twenty keys, three restaurants, a Michelin star, and two private gaming salons — but the original kitchen still serves dinner every night, and the original bar is still where the regulars sit.

From the casino floors to the spa terraces, every room and every ritual is curated by people who have spent their lives perfecting hospitality. Our head sommelier joined us in 2002. Our pastry chef in 2007. The bellman who will meet you at the door has worked here longer than most of our suites have existed. Continuity is not a marketing word for us. It is the entire point.

We have been awarded a five-star rating by every guide that matters, but the recognition we hold most tightly is the one we cannot frame: more than sixty percent of our reservations each year come from guests who have stayed with us before. That is the only number we measure.

A. Laurent
Managing Director

Helpful Answers

Everything you might ask

Is the casino open 24/7?+

Our main gaming floor operates from 14:00 to 04:00 daily. Private salons for high-stakes play can be opened earlier or kept open later by arrangement with your host — overnight private play is not unusual on the weekends. Membership cards are not required for table play; chips can be purchased and cashed at the cage during all open hours.

Is there a dress code?+

Smart casual is the standard after sunset in our dining venues and on the main casino floor. Gentlemen are asked to wear closed shoes and a collared shirt; we will provide a jacket from our cloakroom on request. Beach attire is welcome at the pools and lagoon bars throughout the day. Trainers, beachwear, and swim shorts are not permitted in the main restaurants from 19:00 onwards.

How far is the airport?+

Approximately thirty-five minutes by private car along the coastal road in normal conditions, or fifty-five minutes if your driver takes the scenic inland route through the vineyards (we recommend this on arrival days when there is no hurry). By helicopter from the resort helipad, the journey is twelve minutes door-to-door.

What ages are admitted?+

Our property is reserved for adult guests aged 18 and over, in line with our gaming licence and the character of the resort. Valid government-issued photo identification may be requested on arrival. Children are warmly welcomed at our sister property in the same bay — the concierge will be happy to arrange the booking and transfers between the two estates.

What We Believe

A great resort is felt, not seen.

Anyone can hang a chandelier. Anyone can put marble in a bathroom. What is harder — what takes years — is the muscle memory of a great service team. The pause before they pour. The way the door closes behind them. That is what we have spent a quarter century building. The marble is incidental.

There is a way to do this work and a way not to. We have, over twenty-five years, picked one and stuck to it. Every member of our team — from the front desk to the kitchen porters — has been chosen, trained and kept because they share the same instinct about what good looks like. That is not something a building can produce on its own. It comes from people.

We hope you will come and see for yourself. And, when you do, we hope you will tell us where we got it wrong, so that the next twenty-five years are better than the last.

A. Laurent · M. Bernard
